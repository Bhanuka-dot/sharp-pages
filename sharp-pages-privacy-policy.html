Overview

Sharp Pages ("we," "our," or "us") operates a digital product store selling downloadable resources through Gumroad. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or interact with our content on third-party platforms, including Pinterest.

By using our site or purchasing our products, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect limited information in the following ways:

  • Purchase information — processed entirely by Gumroad. We do not store your payment or billing details directly.
  • Email address — provided when you purchase or sign up, used to deliver products and send updates you've opted into.
  • Usage data — basic analytics such as page views, referral sources, and general location (country/region), collected via standard web analytics tools.
  • Pinterest interaction data — if you interact with our content on Pinterest, Pinterest's own privacy policy governs that data. We may receive aggregate analytics (impressions, saves, clicks) from Pinterest's API.

How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver digital products you purchase
  • To send transactional and product-related emails
  • To understand which content drives traffic and improve our offerings
  • To promote our products on Pinterest via automated and manual pinning
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Pinterest & Third-Party Platforms

We use the Pinterest API to distribute content about our products. Through this integration:

  • We may create and schedule pins featuring our digital products
  • We receive aggregate analytics data (pin impressions, saves, link clicks) — never individual user data
  • We do not collect or store any Pinterest user's personal information
  • Pinterest's use of your data is governed by Pinterest's Privacy Policy

Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential site functionality
  • Analytics (e.g. understanding traffic sources)
  • Affiliate tracking via Gumroad

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Some features may not function properly if you do.

Data Storage & Security

Purchase and payment data is stored and secured by Gumroad. Any email data we hold is stored with industry-standard protections. We do not run our own payment infrastructure.

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority (EU/UK residents)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

Children's Privacy

Sharp Pages is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy
